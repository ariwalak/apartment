Luxury Living, Convenience and Style

Welcome to Time Square apartments in far North Dallas! Our gated community combines the convenience of a central, accessible location with beautiful courtyard views for a peaceful, park-like setting. We are conveniently located less than a mile from the North Dallas Tollway and nearby grocery stores, near the Plano School District, and just minutes from Restaurant Row in Addison. We are about four miles from the restaurants and night life on Belt Line Road and less than four miles to the Willow Bend Mall.

Our homes feature vaulted ceilings, natural light, air conditioning, ceiling fans and a private patio or balcony for some nice, fresh air. Full-size washer and dryer connections, spacious closets, ample storage, energy-saving double-panel windows. Free covered parking(private, detached garages are also available) and a well-equipped modern kitchen are a just few of the features that will save you time and money and make it easier to relax at home.

Time Square Apartment homes are 1 & 2 bedroom homes with vaulted ceilings, are full of natural light, and include outdoor spaces. Our safe and pet-friendly community is in a park-like setting where you can relax and enjoy life Our mission at Time Square Apartment Homes is to provide you with an immaculate apartment, compassionate management, prompt service, and a community that truly feels like home.!
